Welcome to HealthItForward




HealthItForward (formerly Aggregating Outliers) is a web application that collects medical data and identifies factors which may have the most significant effect on the treatment of diseases. It collects this data through a series of surveys that patients fill out detailing how different alternative treatments have impacted their recovery.

This data can be combined and used to find trends among alternative treatments, which can help to identify the specific reasons behind why certain patients cope with a disease better than others. These informal trends can then be studied further using randomized double-blind studies by medical research organizations and labs around the world to officially test their effectiveness.
